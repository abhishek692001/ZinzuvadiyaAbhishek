About Me

I'm Abhishek Zinzuvadiya, a passionate Semiconductor Engineer with a Master's degree in VLSI Design from NIT Surathkal. My expertise lies in digital design and verification, with a focus on creating efficient processor architectures and robust verification environments.

Currently working as a Senior R&D Engineer at Synopsys, I specialize in developing cutting-edge verification methodologies for cache subsystems and processor designs. My work bridges the gap between RTL design and silicon implementation, ensuring optimal performance and power efficiency.

When I'm not working on chip designs, I enjoy mentoring aspiring engineers, contributing to open-source hardware projects, and staying updated with the latest advancements in semiconductor technology.
